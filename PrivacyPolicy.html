1. Introduction


Welcome to BillSetu, a GST invoice generation and management app designed for small businesses, freelancers, and service providers in India. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information BillSetu collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.


By using BillSetu, you agree to the practices described in this policy.




2. Information We Collect


BillSetu is an offline-first application. All data you enter is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that receives or stores your personal information.

    •

Invoice Data

  • Customer name and phone number
    •

App Settings


    

  • Preferred language (English, Hindi, Gujarati, or Marathi)
    • 

  • Currency preference
    • 

  • Invoice number prefix
    • 

  • Theme preference (light or dark)
    • 



2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect


    

  • We do not collect any personal data on our servers.
    •

3. How We Use Your Information


Since all data remains on your device, it is used solely to provide the app's core functionality:


    

  • Generating and displaying GST-compliant invoices
    • 

  • Pre-filling your business details on new invoices
    • 

  • Maintaining a history of your saved invoices

  • Applying your preferred language, currency, and theme settings
    • 

  • Calculating GST amounts using the built-in GST calculator
    • 





4. Advertising


BillSetu displays advertisements to support the free availability of the app. Ads are served through Google AdMob, a third-party advertising platform operated by Google LLC.


4.1 Data Collected by AdMob


To serve relevant ads, Google AdMob may collect and process certain information from your device, including:


    

  • Advertising ID: A resettable, device-level identifier used for ad targeting and frequency capping (Android Advertising ID / Apple IDFA)
    • 

  • IP address: Used to determine approximate geographic location for regional ad targeting
    • 

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and screen size
    • 

  • Ad interaction data: Whether you viewed or tapped an ad
    • 



This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. BillSetu does not receive or store any of this advertising data.


4.2 Ad Types


BillSetu may display the following types of ads:


    

  • Banner ads: Displayed within the app interface
    • 

  • Interstitial ads: Full-screen ads shown at natural transition points within the app
    • 



4.3 Opting Out of Personalized Ads


You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:


    

  • Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
    • 

  • iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
    • 



Opting out does not remove ads from the app; it means the ads you see will be less personalized.




5. Data Storage and Security


All app data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage, a standard React Native local storage mechanism. No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers by BillSetu.


Important considerations:


    

  • Your data is tied to the device on which BillSetu is installed. It is not backed up to the cloud by BillSetu.
    • 

  • If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data (business profile, invoice history, settings) will be permanently deleted.
    • 

  • If you lose or replace your device, your BillSetu data cannot be recovered by us.
    • 

  • The security of locally stored data depends on your device's own security features (e.g., screen lock, device encryption).
    • 



We recommend keeping your device secure and enabling device-level encryption to protect your business data.

7. Sharing of Information


BillSetu does not share your personal or business data with any third parties.


When you use the Share Invoice feature, your invoice is shared directly through your device's native share sheet (e.g., WhatsApp, email, SMS). This sharing is initiated entirely by you and is handled by your device's operating system. BillSetu does not intercept, store, or transmit the shared content.


Advertising-related data (as described in Section 4) is handled solely by Google AdMob under Google's own privacy policy.

9. App Updates


BillSetu may periodically require you to update to the latest version of the app. In some cases, a mandatory (force) update may be required before you can continue using the app. This ensures you have access to the latest features, security patches, and compliance improvements.


When a force update is required, you will be notified within the app and directed to the relevant app store (Google Play Store or Apple App Store) to download the update. The app will not be usable until the update is installed.


We recommend keeping automatic updates enabled on your device to avoid interruptions.
